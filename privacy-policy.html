Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 01, 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Expense Tracker app (“the App”) handles personal information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information you manually enter to use its features:

  • Expense Data: Amount, category (e.g., food, bills, savings), short description, and payment method.
  • Wishlist Data: Item name, estimated amount, target date, priority level, short description, and notes.

No personal identifiers such as name, email address, or location are required to use this app.

2. How Information Is Used

All personal data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not send, transmit, or back up this information to any remote server or cloud service. As developers, we do not have access to any of your personal or spending-related data.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

You can edit or delete any data within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

All information is stored securely on your local device storage. You are solely responsible for managing your data, including backing it up or deleting it. Please be aware that if your device is lost or the data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

5. Third-Party Services

Expense Tracker does not use or integrate any third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase
  • Advertising or marketing platforms (e.g., AdMob)
  • Cloud storage or syncing systems

While the app itself does not collect or transmit user data, please note that Google Play may collect basic information about app usage, device type, and crash reports. This information is handled by Google under their own Privacy Policy and is not accessible to the app developer.

6. No Internet Connection Required

The App is designed to function offline and does not require an internet connection for its core features. No user data is transmitted over the internet by the App itself.

7. Permissions We Request

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: Used to remind users to record expenses or wishlist updates (intervals such as 2 hours, 6 hours, or 8 hours).

All permissions are requested only when necessary and are never used for background tracking or data collection.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children.
